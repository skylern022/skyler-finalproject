Give it up for Queen!

"We do things in a style that is very different to anybody else" ~ Queen

Queen formed in 1971, and signed their first contract in 1973 with EMI. Since then they have gone on to become rock royalty, pun intended, with iconic songs such as "Bohemian Rhapsody" and "We Are the Champions."
